Missing Div: Story so far

Web Developments Hottest new Band

Fusing Industrial Polish Break-beats, Translucent African Poly-rhythms and the Mystique of Irish Nursery rhyme with an eclectic love of Dance, Tech, Rockno, Post-punk, Jazz-light, Death-Metal, Flex-box, Grime, Hip-Hop, Opacity, Justify Content and everything in the space-between, Missing Div {}© have hit the Web Dev music scene like a Runaway train in 2022. Once Rock and Rolls best kept secret the cat is now well and truly out of the bag as Missing Div's hordes of fanatical fans (The Div-ards and Div-ettes) grow by the day

Long revered and idolised in underground Dark Web forums, Missing Div {}© have really invaded popular culture in the last 3 months. Their Ear-worm classic Push and Pull {till your Git Hub is full} has proven to be the Sleeper hit of 2022. Hitting the number 2 spot on Apples Top ringtones in March and remaining in the top 10 for 13 days. In April it hit the top ten in both Japan and North Korea where it has been adopted as a counter-culture anthem by teenagers down the skate park

Success hasn't changed the Missing Div {}©Aaron, Iwo, Nigel and the other one remain as humble, focused and grounded today as they did when the sweet angel of fortune tossed them all together in a virtual room what feels like years ago. Allegations of trashed internet chatrooms, poor Zoom etiquette and plagiarism have so far been brushed off. The mission remains the same. Take over the world with the power, purpose and life-affirming joy of their songs. Missing Div {}© 4life xxxx
